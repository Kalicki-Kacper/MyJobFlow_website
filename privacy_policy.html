Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 26, 2023

1. Overview

Your privacy is our primary concern. MyJobFlow was designed to be a private tool for your personal use. We do not collect, sell, or view your personal job application data.

2. Data Storage (iCloud & CloudKit)

All data you enter into MyJobFlow is stored in a private container in your personal iCloud account (Apple's CloudKit). This means:

  • We (the developers) cannot access, read, or modify your data.
  • Your data is synced securely across your devices using your Apple ID.
  • If you delete the app or your iCloud data, the data is removed permanently.

3. Analytics

We use anonymous analytics tools to help us improve the application's stability and performance. We track:

  • App crashes and error logs.
  • Aggregate usage metrics (e.g., "70% of users use Dark Mode").

We never track the content of your job applications, notes, or salary information.

4. Third-Party Sharing

We do not share, sell, or rent your data to any third-party advertisers or data brokers.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us via our Support page.
