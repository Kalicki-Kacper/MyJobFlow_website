Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data secure?

Yes. Your data lives in your private iCloud database. We cannot see it.

How do I sync between devices?

Ensure you are signed into the same iCloud account on all devices and that iCloud Drive is enabled for MyJobFlow.

Can I export my data?

Yes, go to Settings > Export Data to generate a CSV file of your applications.
